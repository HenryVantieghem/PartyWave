# PartyWave Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: [DATE]

Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how PartyWave ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information**:
- Email address
- Full name
- Profile photo (optional)
- Date of birth (for age verification)
- Phone number (if verified)

**Profile Information**:
- Bio/description
- Interests
- Location (city/neighborhood)
- Social links (optional)

**Content You Create**:
- Parties you create or join
- Crews you create or join
- Photos and memories you upload
- Messages you send
- Quick plans and votes
- Ratings and reviews

### Information We Collect Automatically

**Device Information**:
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information

**Usage Information**:
- App features you use
- Screens you view
- Time spent in app
- Interactions (taps, swipes)
- Crash reports and errors

**Location Information**:
- Approximate location (city-level)
- Precise location (when you create/join parties)
- Location is ONLY used for finding nearby parties and crews

**Technical Information**:
- IP address
- Browser type
- App version
- Time zone

## How We Use Your Information

### Provide and Improve Our Services

- Create and manage your account
- Show you nearby parties and crews
- Enable real-time updates and notifications
- Personalize your experience
- Provide customer support
- Analyze and improve app features
- Fix bugs and prevent fraud

### Safety and Security

- Verify your identity
- Enforce our Terms of Service
- Detect and prevent abuse
- Investigate suspicious activity
- Protect user safety

### Communications

- Send you service updates
- Respond to your questions
- Send push notifications (if enabled)
- Email newsletters (opt-in only)

### Legal Compliance

- Comply with legal obligations
- Respond to legal requests
- Enforce our rights

## How We Share Your Information

### Public Information

**Visible to All Users**:
- Public profile (name, photo, bio)
- Public parties you create
- Public crews you join
- Public ratings and reviews

**Visible to Crew Members**:
- Crew activity
- Quick plans and votes
- Messages within crew

**Visible to Party Attendees**:
- Attendance at public parties
- Check-in status
- Party memories you upload

### Service Providers

We share data with trusted third parties who help us operate:

- **Supabase** (database hosting)
- **AWS S3** (photo storage)
- **Expo** (app infrastructure)
- **Sentry** (error tracking)
- **Analytics providers** (Firebase, etc.)

All providers are contractually required to protect your data.

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government request, or to protect safety and rights.

### Business Transfers

If PartyWave is acquired or merged, your data may be transferred. We'll notify you before this happens.

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access and Control

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and data
- Export your data
- Opt-out of marketing emails
- Control notification preferences

**To exercise these rights**: Settings → Privacy → Data Rights

### Location Control

You can control location access:
- iOS: Settings → Privacy → Location Services → PartyWave
- Android: Settings → Apps → PartyWave → Permissions → Location

### Cookie Control (Web Version)

We use cookies for:
- Authentication
- Preferences
- Analytics

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

## Data Retention

**Active Accounts**: We retain your data as long as your account is active.

**Deleted Accounts**: Most data deleted within 30 days. Some data retained for:
- Legal compliance: 7 years
- Safety/fraud prevention: 1 year
- Aggregated analytics: Indefinitely (anonymized)

## Children's Privacy

PartyWave is for users 17+. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 17. If we learn we've collected data from someone under 17, we'll delete it immediately.

## International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your residence, including the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

## Data Security

We protect your data with:
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest
- Access controls
- Regular security audits
- Employee training

However, no system is 100% secure. Use a strong password and enable two-factor authentication.

## Third-Party Links
